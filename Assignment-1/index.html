Do I Wanna Know?

Arctic Monkeys • Album: AM • 2013

About The Song

I chose the song Do I Wanna Know? by Arctic Monkeys. The song is about emotional uncertainty. The wanting of someone who might not feel the same way, and the fact that you may not want to know if they feel the same way. It's a song that when you truly listen to the lyrics, you will hear a deeper story than just someone who is in love with someone who may not love them back.

About Arctic Monkeys

Arctic Monkeys is an English rock band that was founded in Sheffield in 2002. They are known for deep-meaning lyrics and energetic performances. The band consists of Alex Turner, Matt Helders, Jamie Cook, and Nick O’Malley. Andy Nicholson was a founding member of Arctic Monkeys as a bassist but left in 2006. The band became widely popular for their unique sound and storytelling style, which is strongly shown throughout the AM album.
